QUASAR: Datasets for Question Answering by Search and Reading
Bhuwan Dhingra, Kathryn Mazaitis, William Cohen (arxiv, 9 August 2017), 10 cites, 3 links
Problem Need better and bigger question answering datasets that provide context with mix of relevant information and noise.
Type Dataset.
Solution The paper introduces two new datasets. First, QUASAR-S is for fill-in-the-blank questions with data from Stack Overflow. The questions are the tag definitions with missed tags (might be multiple questions for one definition since it might have other tags mentioned inside). The dataset also provides context (relevant posts and comments) as well as annotations about relation of head tag (the one that has the definition) and missed tag (the one omitted which is not necessary the same as head tag). The second dataset, QUASAR-T is similar, but contains open-domain trivia questions. The answers in this case are not the missed entries, but any general sentences. There are also context and annotations (what kind of question) for this dataset.
Methods MF-e, MF-i, Richardson et al., 2013/SW, Richardson et al., 2013/SW+D
Results QUASAR-T (precision, recall). MF-e (0.20, 0.43), MF-i (0.50, 0.32), Richardson et al., 2013/SW (0.93, 0.99), Richardson et al., 2013/SW+D (0.61, 0.41)

QUASAR-S (precision, recall). MF-e (0.20, 0.43), MF-i (0.50, 0.32), Richardson et al., 2013/SW (0.92, 0.99), Richardson et al., 2013/SW+D (0.69, 0.41)
